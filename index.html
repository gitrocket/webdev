about

I have a Bachelor of Science in Psychology with an emphasis in Statistics and Research Methodology. After college, I traveled to Prague and taught English as a second language for a year.

Since then, I have worked as a freelance photojournalist for numerous news gathering organizations including the Associated Press, The New York Times, USA Today, the Wall Street Journal, Bloomberg News, and Education Week.

I have always loved technology. What drew me into web development was a problem I needed to solve as a photographer. I was looking for a stand-alone keyword harvester, but couldn't find anything off-the-shelf, so I built one using Python and its Natural Language Toolkit library. The excitement that came with building something from nothing, that solved a problem, was what gave me the impetus to pursue software and web development.

If you are looking to hire an innovative and resourceful developer, please reach out. I am always excited to work with other developers to create problem solving applications.
